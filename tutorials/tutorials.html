The first thing I usually do in my "controller design workflow" is to model the system with differential equations. Then I try to get an understanding of the behavior of the system, by simulating the open loop plant. This part often serves as a check to see if I modelled correctly and if the modelling depth is adequate. The next step is to close the loop and develop a control strategy. Optimization plays a roll in all the steps. I often use optimization methods to find suitable trajectories to transit between operating points.
