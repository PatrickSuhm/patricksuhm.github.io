solar bike trailer

Ever since I built my first e-bike in 2007, I dreamed of powering my bike with solar. In recent years, the efficiency and price of solar modules have developed so advantageously that this is now possible and useful. So I put a 100W panel onto a bike trailer. The module automatically aligns itself with the sun. This is achieved by two brightness sensors that point in opposing directions. The panel is then tilted in the direction of the brighter sensor with the help of a linear actuator:

The panel is charging the e-bike battery that is mounted to the trailer. I also added a motor to the back wheel of the trailer, so that it converts any ordinary bike to an e-bike. The speed of the motor can be controlled by a custom app or the crank velocity:
